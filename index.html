About Us

Welcome to Octagon Shopfitting and Bespoke Joinery, where over two decades of expertise meet innovation and excellence in the heart of London and its surroundings. Our journey began over 20 years ago, rooted in a passion for creating spaces that combine aesthetic appeal with functionality. Today, we are proud to be at the forefront of shopfitting and bespoke joinery services, continually pushing the boundaries of design and craftsmanship.
With a commitment to safety and quality, we boast 10 years of specialized experience in manufacturing Fire Doors, proudly approved by BM Trada. Our dedication to adhering to the highest standards ensures that our products are not only visually captivating but also meet rigorous safety specifications.
At Octagon, innovation is at the core of what we do. We are constantly exploring new designs, materials, and technologies to bring our clients' visions to life. From the initial concept to the final touches, our team works tirelessly to create bespoke solutions that are tailored to each project's unique requirements.
Our expertise spans a diverse range of sectors, including health, retail, airlines, schools, colleges, and offices. This versatility enables us to understand the specific needs of each industry, providing tailored solutions that enhance functionality and enrich the user experience.
Octagon Shopfitting and Bespoke Joinery is more than just a service provider; we are your partner in transforming spaces. Let us bring your vision to life with our commitment to excellence, innovation, and craftsmanship.

Our Clients

At Octagon Shopfitting and Bespoke Joinery, we pride ourselves on the relationships we've built and the projects we've brought to life alongside an impressive roster of clients. From bustling retail spaces and serene health facilities to dynamic educational institutions and cutting-edge offices, our portfolio is a testament to our versatility and commitment to excellence.
